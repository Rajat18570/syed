ABOUT US


The mission of Syed Sir Classes is to inspire the young minds with scientific acumen to be industry ready with clear fundamentals, so that they can contribute to the welfare of our society diligently. The students here are motivated to develop an analytical mind to grasp and analyze the situations, hence life balancing techniques are imparted indirectly.

Out of thousands of tuition centres and coaching institutes promising a bright future, why choose us? Syed Sir Classes stands out amidst the stream of conventional institutes in more ways than one. The teaching methods adopted by us ensure that a child gets one to one interaction and learning opportunities even in a classroom environment. Syed Sir Classes is an institute in Delhi which caters to academic and personality growth in a child. With an experience of over 20 years, we are constantly working to create a student-friendly environment which ensures that each student finds his/her place in the class. The courses are designed with an exclusive Artificial Intelligence training program that curates questions to cater to the individual needs of a child. It also ensures that each student's progress is directly linked to the teacher's vision.The classroom environment is healthy, humorous and light to ensure that academics are not reinforced as a punishment. Above all, a healthy and career-oriented mindset is developed in a matter of months under our vigilance.

Why us?

Syed Sir Classes provides one-to-one interaction and tailored learning experiences, ensuring individual student needs are met.
The institute's exclusive Artificial Intelligence program adapts courses to students' unique requirements, linking progress directly to the teacher's vision for a dynamic educational journey.
Beyond academics, Syed Sir Classes fosters a positive classroom environment, emphasizing personality growth and life-balancing techniques, quickly cultivating a healthy, career-oriented mindset.
